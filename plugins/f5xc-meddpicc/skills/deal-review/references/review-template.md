# Weekly Deal Review — Question Template

Use these questions to inspect each MEDDPICC element during weekly
deal reviews. Not every question applies to every deal — focus on
what's most relevant to the current stage and the gaps identified
in the last review.

---

## Metrics

- What quantified business outcome does the customer expect?
- Do we have a confirmed baseline and target?
- How will the customer measure success?
- Who owns these metrics on the customer side?
- Has the customer validated our value model?

## Economic Buyer

- Who is the EB and have we confirmed their budget authority?
- When did we last interact directly with the EB?
- Do we have the next EB meeting scheduled?
- What does the EB personally care about?
- Is the EB actively sponsoring this through procurement?

## Decision Criteria

- What are the ranked evaluation criteria?
- Have we influenced criteria toward our strengths?
- Are all key stakeholders aligned on the criteria?
- What criteria could disqualify us?
- How do we score vs. competition on each criterion?

## Decision Process

- What is the next step in the customer's decision process?
- Who owns that step and what's the target date?
- Are there any new gates or stakeholders we didn't know about?
- Is the timeline tracking to the MAP?
- What could cause a delay?

## Paper Process

- What procurement steps remain?
- Have we submitted required security artifacts?
- What is the status of legal review?
- Is the quote format confirmed?
- What is the expected timeline from "yes" to signature?

## Identify Pain

- Is the pain still urgent? Has anything changed?
- Has new pain surfaced since our last review?
- Is there a deadline driver or triggering event?
- Do multiple stakeholders still agree on the pain?
- What's the consequence of inaction in the next 90 days?

## Champion

- What concrete action did our champion take this week?
- Did they follow through on last week's commitment?
- Have they shared any new internal intelligence?
- Are they still actively advocating?
- If no action — is this person really our champion?

## Competition

- Has the competitive landscape changed?
- Did we learn anything new about other vendors' positioning?
- Is "do nothing" or "build internally" gaining traction?
- Have we executed any differentiation tests?
- What's our win theme, and is it still valid?

---

## Red Flags to Watch For

- Champion hasn't taken action in 2+ weeks
- No EB access scheduled or planned
- Decision Process dates slipping without explanation
- New stakeholders appearing late in the cycle
- Customer avoiding Metrics discussions
- "We'll get back to you" without a specific date
- POV running past its exit criteria date
- Paper Process hasn't started in late-stage deal
