# MEDDPICC Scoring Rubric

Detailed criteria for scoring each element 0–4.

---

## Metrics (M)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
| ----- | ----- | -------- |
| 4 | **Committed** | Customer has committed to metrics in writing or proposal. Baseline, target, measurement method, and timeline all documented and agreed. |
| 3 | **Quantified** | Customer-stated baseline AND target with measurement method and timeline. Metrics tied to executive priority and budget justification. |
| 2 | **Validated** | KPIs documented and confirmed with at least one stakeholder. Baseline or target identified but not both. |
| 1 | **Identified** | Qualitative statements only ("improve security", "reduce costs"). No baseline or measurement method. Seller-hypothesized metrics. |
| 0 | **Unknown** | No metrics discussion has occurred. |

---

## Economic Buyer (E)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
| ----- | ----- | -------- |
| 4 | **Sponsoring** | EB is actively sponsoring the deal through procurement. Direct access achieved. EB priorities and personal win documented. |
| 3 | **Aligned** | Named individual with confirmed budget authority. Direct access achieved (meeting held or scheduled). EB priorities documented. |
| 2 | **Engaged** | EB identified by name and title. No direct access yet but plan exists. Champion confirms EB's priorities. |
| 1 | **Identified** | Role identified but not the specific person. Access only through intermediaries. Assumptions about budget authority. |
| 0 | **Unknown** | Unknown who controls budget. No plan to identify or access. |

---

## Decision Criteria (D)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
| ----- | ----- | -------- |
| 4 | **Locked** | Criteria influenced toward your strengths. Key stakeholders aligned on criteria. Competitive position strong on top-priority items. |
| 3 | **Differentiated** | Written criteria from customer with priority ranking. Your competitive position mapped per criterion. Influence actions taken. |
| 2 | **Validated** | Criteria partially documented. Priority ranking informal. Some influence actions taken. Not all stakeholders aligned. |
| 1 | **Identified** | General requirements known but not documented or ranked. No influence actions taken. Single contact's view only. |
| 0 | **Unknown** | Don't know how the customer will evaluate solutions. |

---

## Decision Process (D)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
| ----- | ----- | -------- |
| 4 | **Controlled** | All gates mapped with no surprises expected. Legal/security/procurement flow confirmed. Artifacts submitted at each gate. |
| 3 | **Validated** | Step-by-step process documented with named owners, dates, and required artifacts. General timeline confirmed. |
| 2 | **Mapped** | Major steps identified but dates or owners missing. Some gates unclear. General timeline known. |
| 1 | **Identified** | High-level understanding only ("they'll do a POV then decide"). No dates or named owners. |
| 0 | **Unknown** | Unknown how the customer will make and approve the decision. |

---

## Paper Process (P)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
| ----- | ----- | -------- |
| 4 | **Complete** | All procurement, legal, and security steps done. Signature workflow mapped. Quote/PO format confirmed. Payment terms agreed. |
| 3 | **In Progress** | Procurement steps documented with owners and timelines. Security artifacts submitted. Legal terms in review. |
| 2 | **Mapped** | Some procurement steps known. Security requirements identified but artifacts not yet submitted. Legal terms not yet reviewed. |
| 1 | **Identified** | Aware that procurement exists but steps unknown. No artifacts staged. Assuming it will be "straightforward." |
| 0 | **Unknown** | No procurement discovery done. |

---

## Implicate the Pain (I)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
| ----- | ----- | -------- |
| 4 | **Urgent** | Multiple stakeholders confirm the pain. Triggering event identified. Clear deadline driver. Consequence of inaction is severe and imminent. |
| 3 | **Quantified** | Pain quantified with business impact. Consequence of inaction documented. General urgency without specific deadline. |
| 2 | **Acknowledged** | Pain acknowledged by customer but not fully quantified. Single stakeholder confirmation. |
| 1 | **Identified** | Pain implied but not explicitly confirmed by customer. Seller's interpretation of the problem. No urgency driver. |
| 0 | **Unknown** | No pain identified or discussed. Solution looking for a problem. |

---

## Champion (C)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
| ----- | ----- | -------- |
| 4 | **Mobilizing** | Champion is actively coaching you, sharing internal intelligence, and taking concrete actions weekly (EB intros, internal advocacy, competitor intel). |
| 3 | **Validated** | Named individual with influence and credibility. Personal win documented. Has taken concrete action recently. |
| 2 | **Tested** | Friendly contact with some influence. Willing to help but hasn't taken significant action yet. Personal win unclear. |
| 1 | **Identified** | Friendly contact without organizational power. Passive supporter. No concrete actions taken. |
| 0 | **Unknown** | No internal advocate identified. |

---

## Competition (C)

| Score | Label | Criteria |
| ----- | ----- | -------- |
| 4 | **Neutralized** | Competitive proof delivered. Customer perception documented and favorable. "Do nothing" risk assessed. Win theme validated with customer. |
| 3 | **Differentiated** | Competitors named and qualified. Customer's perception of each alternative documented. Differentiation strategy clear. |
| 2 | **Assessed** | Competitors identified but customer's perception unclear. General differentiation known. No competitive proof planned. |
| 1 | **Identified** | Assumptions about competition. "We don't think they're talking to anyone." No differentiation strategy. |
| 0 | **Unknown** | No competitive intelligence gathered. |

---

## Scoring Guidelines

- **26–32 (~81–100%):** Strong deal — Green. Forecast as Commit if Decision Process and Paper Process are both ≥3.
- **14–25 (~44–78%):** Developing deal — Yellow. Forecast as Best Case with clear gap-closing plan.
- **0–13 (~0–41%):** At risk — Red. Forecast as Pipeline; significant work needed or qualify out.

**Overall score formula:** `(sum of 8 element scores / 32) × 100`
